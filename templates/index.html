Before you begin
  • Individual texts range from a few hundred KB to ~19 MB each. Large batches may take several minutes — keep this tab open.
  • Lists longer than {{ batch_size }} IDs will be split into multiple zip files and downloaded one at a time automatically.
  • Your CSV should have one TCP ID per row in the first column (e.g. A00001, B21453). A header row is fine.
  • Any IDs that cannot be found will be listed in an errors.txt inside the zip.
